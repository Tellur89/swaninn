About Us

An inviting pub & restaurant serving seasonal menus, tempting cocktails, well-kept real ales & fine wines located in the picturesque village of Chappel. Surrounded by beautiful countryside & under the constant gaze of the striking viaduct the Swan Inn is a Grade II listed building with courtyard, gardens, al fresco terrace & heated marquee which are perfect for all day dining & unwinding.

Whether you just fancy breakfast or a pub lunch, have a meeting with business colleagues for coffee, are planning a special night or simply having drinks with friends, the Swan Inn is the perfect choice.

Modern pub food & fantastic Chef's Specials are made from scratch using the finest, freshest ingredients hand sourced from Billingsgate, Smithfield & Spitalfields Market in addition to using local suppliers. In addition to our in-house dining options, we also offer Take Away & Delivery including our popular Sunday Roasts.

Drinks to complement our food include cocktails complete with seasonal specials, draught beers & lagers, Real Ale, wines from around the world including eight by the glass, a selection of soft drinks, coffees & teas.

The Swan Inn also holds weekly 2 For 1 deals, offers the best Sunday Roasts, Food Theme Events, Children's Menu, Beer Festivals & more making it the ideal family local.

What's on?



Enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner with a warm welcome, tempting cocktails, fine wines, well- kept ales & seasonal homemade menus here at the Swan Inn, Chappel.

Our reservations diary is open for bookings inside where you can enjoy full table service or in our heated marquee where food & drinks can be ordered from the bar via your phone – either way it couldn’t be easier to enjoy a safe visit out with friends & family



Gift Cards


Our gift cards are the perfect present for birthdays, anniversaries or simply as a way to say “thank you.” Contact the pub on 01787 222 353 to purchase yours.

Loyalty Scheme


Pick up one of our loyalty cards and enjoy 5p for every £1 spent on Food or Drink either in house or take away on your Loyalty Card which can be redeemed against your bill at any time. Please pick yours up on your next visit.

Functions


Thinking of having a party? The Swan Inn caters for all kinds of events, from intimate occasions in our private dining room to larger celebrations in our Garden Room or Marquee. Our bright stylishly decorated pub is the perfect choice for any gathering and we can guide you from initial enquiries through to the event itself.
